About Me

I am Suwijak Promsatid, a passionate undergraduate student in Information Technology at KMITL and an active participant in the Young Webmasters Camp 20 on a programming major.

I have a strong interest in web development and am always eager to explore new technologies. My passion is to create user-friendly websites that simplify complex ideas and make them accessible to everyone.

I actively participate in tech events and meetups such as BKKjs and Stupid Hackathon. I am also an active contributor to various activities at IT KMITL, including the IT KMITL Open House, TobeIT, and IT Camp, etc.

Additionally, my friends and I founded a technology club called "Creasy Club," inspired by Creatorsgarten, with the goal of sharing knowledge and experiences with others. We have organized several tech conferences and plan to host many more activities in the future.
